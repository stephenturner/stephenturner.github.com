Stephen Turner

Im a bioinformatician and data scientist at Signature Science, LLC. in Charlottesville, Virginia. After getting an M.S. in statistics I completed a Ph.D.in human genetics and a postdoctoral fellowship in epidemiology. I founded and directed University of Virginias Bioinformatics core from 2011-2019, and have been a science & technology advisor at Signature Science ever since. I love talking about genomics, bioinformatics, human identification, forensics, running, and homebrewing.
